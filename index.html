Why Us?

Supported Simulators

We support all flight sims with a global map. Our goal is to provide assistance and resources for enthusiasts of virtual aviation, ensuring a seamless and immersive experience across various platforms.

Flight Freedom!

In our Virtual Airlines, we've hopped aboard the Chartered Flight System, giving our pilots the oppurtunity to fly on any routes they want! No more strict routes; now they can dance with the clouds as they please and the sky will be theirs'. It's like giving a pilot wings and a disco ball!

Inclusive Wings

Our Story

Flymoon VA took its inaugural flight on May 2nd, 2023, embarking on a journey that initially revolved around GEOFS, a virtual flight simulator. However, fueled by an insatiable passion for aviation and a commitment to inclusivity, Flymoon quickly transcended its initial confines, broadening its horizons to include all simulators boasting global map integration. This expansion marked the beginning of a remarkable transformation, as Flymoon blossomed from its humble origins into a flourishing community, a testament to the shared love for aviation that unites enthusiasts from every corner of the globe. As the Flymoon family continues to grow and evolve, its commitment to fostering camaraderie, exploration, and the pursuit of dreams remains unwavering, propelling it ever forward into the boundless skies of possibility.

Flymoon VA lives and breathes the ethos of "Chase Your Dream," a rallying cry that encapsulates our relentless pursuit of excellence and adventure in the virtual skies. From the very outset, our journey has been marked by the unwavering dedication of our passionate pilots and committed staff. Their collective efforts have been instrumental in propelling us from humble beginnings to the thriving community we are today. Along the way, we've encountered challenges and triumphs, each serving as valuable lessons that have enriched our experience and strengthened our resolve. As we continue to soar to new heights, our commitment to empowering individuals to pursue their aviation dreams remains unwavering, driving us forward with boundless enthusiasm and determination.
